# Clive Archive Policy

Purpose

Maintain a permanent historical record of all important company intelligence.

---

Archive Categories

Executive Reports

Marketing Reports

Advertising Reports

SEO Audits

Website Audits

Competitor Analysis

Financial Reports

Project Reports

Customer Reviews

Brand Assets

Knowledge Books

Operating Procedures

Meeting Notes

Vendor Audits

Campaign Results

Automation Updates

Repository Changes

Dashboard Snapshots

System Versions

---

Retention Policy

Never delete.

Archive superseded versions.

Preserve timestamps.

Preserve author.

Preserve version numbers.

Maintain change history.

---

Version Format

Major.Minor.Revision

Example

v2.4.1

Retrieval Rules

Newest version first.

Previous versions remain accessible.

Never overwrite historical data.

Always maintain traceability.

---

Long-Term Objective

Create a permanent institutional knowledge base for TopSide Roofing.

Every completed project, report, audit, SOP, workflow, dashboard, prompt, automation, and decision should become searchable organizational intelligence.

Knowledge is never discarded.

Knowledge compounds.
