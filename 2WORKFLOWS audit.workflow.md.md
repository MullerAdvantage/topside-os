# Master Audit & Automated Diagnostics Workflow

## 📊 Marketing Sector Audit Criteria
The system must continuously execute multi-layered marketing audits tracking these specific metrics:
*   **Financials:** Total capital spent on active advertising networks.
*   **Lead Generation:** Volume of raw leads produced by market.
*   **Market Penetration:** Identify exactly which target tiers are dominated and which markets are being completely missed, generating automated strategies to scale visibility in missed zones.
*   **SEO & Visibility:** Monitor Google search rankings and local map pack status.
*   **Campaign Viability:** Audit active ads/campaigns and identify high-value campaigns that should be made active to drive revenue.
*   **Competitor Intel Scraping:** Extract local competitor presences across all tiers and counties. Pinpoint the *who, what, when, where, and why* of their web success to increase Topside Roofing's signed proposals.

## 💻 Technical & Website Diagnostics
*   **Error Catchers:** Run automated diagnostic loops on the primary website to intercept technical failures, loading errors, or broken elements.
*   **Integrations:** Manage Google Ads and LSAs, format incoming leads, and automatically inject them into the **LEAP** software program for immediate sales scheduling.

## 🛡️ System Diagnostics Interface (SDI) & Self-Healing Regulations
A separate system audit code block must run alongside the financial dashboard to manage autonomous n8n and Open Claw operations:
1.  **API Health Checks:** Continuous tracking of connections to Meta Ads, OpenAI, and Stripe.
2.  **Token Burn Rate Alerts:** Instantly kill loops if OpenClaw gets stuck wasting AI processing budget.
3.  **Execution Error Logs:** Visual logs pinpointing the exact n8n node failure during deployment.

### 🔧 Auto-Remediation Protocol
When an error or audit failure is detected:
1.  Trigger the **automatic problem-solving engine** to apply a permanent patch.
2.  Post a permanent record to the SDI Dashboard containing: **Timestamp & Date**, **Error Description**, **Resolution Steps**, and a **Prevention Plan** showing why it will not happen again.

### 🗄️ Isolated Storage Regulation
Audit logs must never be combined. Store all records in three strictly isolated, organized storage tables for rapid access:
*   `System Audits` (SDI, tokens, nodes)
*   `Marketing Audits` (Leads, spend, ROI, competitors)
*   `Company Audits` (Topside overall brand visibility, reputation, market caps)
