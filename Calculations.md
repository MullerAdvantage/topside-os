# Calculations

## Standard Formula Library

- Conversion Rate = (Conversions / Leads) × 100
- Close Rate = (Signed Contracts / Estimates) × 100
- Customer Acquisition Cost = Marketing Spend / New Customers
- Return on Ad Spend = Revenue Attributed / Ad Spend
- Marketing ROI = ((Marketing Revenue - Marketing Cost) / Marketing Cost) × 100
- Gross Margin = (Revenue - Direct Costs) / Revenue × 100
- Net Profit Margin = Net Profit / Revenue × 100
- Job Cycle Time = Completion Date - Contract Date
- Customer Satisfaction = Average Survey Score
- Health Score = Weighted KPI scoring (0–100)

## Dashboard Specific
Customize KPI weights for the Sales dashboard based on business priorities.
