# Executive Dashboard

## Purpose
CEO command center providing a complete real-time overview of company health.

## Primary Users
- Business Owner
- Executive Team
- Department Managers

## Objectives
- Present live business intelligence
- Monitor KPI health
- Detect issues automatically
- Recommend corrective actions
- Track trends over time

## Core Files
- Prompt.md
- Metrics.md (future)
- Calculations.md (future)
- DataSources.md (future)
- Reports.md (future)

## Standard KPI Fields
- Current Value
- Previous Value
- Target
- Benchmark
- Variance
- Trend
- Health Score
- Health Status
- Financial Impact
- AI Recommendation
- Priority
- Data Source
- Last Updated

## Required Data Sources
- Google Analytics 4
- Google Search Console
- Google Ads
- Google Business Profile
- Local Services Ads
- LEAP CRM
- QuickBooks
- CallRail
- Internal Company Database

## AI Outputs
- Executive Summary
- Critical Alerts
- Opportunities
- Recommended Actions
- 30/60/90 Day Forecast
